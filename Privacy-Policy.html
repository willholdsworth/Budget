Budget Base

Privacy Policy - Last Updated: July 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Budget Base, the minimalist budgeting application developed by Will Holdsworth ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("Budget Base" or the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information.

Summary of Key Points

  • The App stores all data locally on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your financial data on any external servers.
  • No account creation or login is required to use the App.
  • You can export your budget data as an encoded text file for backup purposes.
  • If you delete or uninstall the App, your data cannot be recovered.
  • The App operates entirely offline with no internet connectivity requirements.

App Functionality and Data Handling

Budget Base is designed with simplicity and privacy in mind. The App provides the following features, all of which operate locally on your device:

  • Creation and management of weekly and monthly budgets
  • Income and expense tracking
  • Savings goals monitoring
  • Multiple budget views and reporting
  • Data export functionality

Local Storage of Information

All data you input into the App, including:

  • Budget categories
  • Income entries
  • Expense records
  • Savings goals
  • Budget settings and preferences

is stored exclusively on your device's local storage using secure, native data storage methods. We do not transmit any of this information to our servers or any third-party servers.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, use, or share any personal information about you, your finances, or your device. The App functions entirely offline and does not connect to the internet to send or receive any of your data. There are no analytics, tracking, or monitoring systems in place.

Export Feature

The App provides a feature that allows you to export your budget data as an encoded .txt file. This exported file remains under your control, and you decide if and with whom to share it. We do not have access to these exported files. This feature exists solely to provide you with backup capabilities and the ability to transfer your data between devices if desired.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on the security measures you have implemented on your device. We recommend using standard security practices such as:

  • Setting a strong device passcode
  • Enabling biometric authentication if available
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Not sharing your device with others who shouldn't have access to your financial information

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains stored on your device until you choose to delete it through the App or uninstall the App entirely. Once the App is uninstalled, all associated data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. We do not maintain any backups of your data on our servers, as we never receive or store your data in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Budget Base is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
